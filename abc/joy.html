Home Page – Course Introduction

Welcome to the world of Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA)! This course is all about learning how to organize and process data effectively to solve problems faster and smarter.

What is the course about?

This course teaches us how to store and manage data (data structures) and how to create step-by-step methods (algorithms) to solve different problems. It’s the foundation of computer programming and is used in almost every software and app you can think of.

What kind of data structures and algorithms have you studied?

Data Structures

•   Arrays (like lists of items), Stacks (like a stack of plates), Queues (like a line of people), and Linked Lists (like a chain).

•   Advanced ones like Trees (family trees) and Graphs (maps with points and connections).

Algorithms

•   Sorting and Searching: How to organize data (like sorting numbers) or quickly find what you need (like finding a name in a contact list).

•   Pathfinding: Finding the shortest route, like Google Maps does.

•   Optimization: Solving problems by making the best choices, like packing a bag with maximum items but limited space.
